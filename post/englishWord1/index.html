symbol

Expain: a mark or character used as a conventional representation of an object, function, or process, e.g. the letter or letters standing for a chemical element or a character in musical notation.

synonym: sign character mark letter hieroglyph

antonym:

verb :symbolize

syllable

ˈsiləb(ə)l

Expain :a unit of pronunciation having one vowel sound, with or without surrounding consonants, forming the whole or a part of a word; e.g., there are two syllables in water and three in inferno.

verb: pronounce (a word or phrase) clearly, syllable by syllable.

symbolically

simˈbälək(ə)lē

Expain:purely in terms of what is being represented or implied.

象征性地

example :the duration of the Great Work was expressed symbolically as 12 days

symbolism

ˈsimbəˌlizəm 象征主义

Expain:the use of symbols to represent ideas or qualities. (noun)
eg:

  • the use of symbols to represent ideas or qualities.
  • he has always believed in the importance of **symbolism** in garden art
